# Delivery-as-a-Service Decision Register

This register separates approved product baselines from decisions that remain configurable or unresolved. It records only what the delivery documents support; an open item is not a recommendation or an implied final value.

## Status and ownership

- **Approved** — confirmed in the product definition or established by an authoritative delivery contract.
- **Open — configurable** — the capability is approved, but an operating value or rule must be selected and may vary by tenant, city, zone, mode, or environment.
- **Open — business** — product or operating policy requires an accountable business decision.
- **Open — legal** — legal, regulatory, contractual, compliance, or privacy review is required before policy is finalized.
- **Deferred** — intentionally assigned to a later approved phase; details can remain open until that phase is designed.

The **owner category** names the function accountable for driving the decision, not a specific person.

## Review rule

When an open item is resolved, record the selected rule in the relevant product or contract document, change its status here to **Approved**, add the approving owner category and source, and preserve the rationale and consequences. Do not close an item solely because an implementation has chosen a default.
